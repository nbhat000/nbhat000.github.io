Our Story

A SERIES OF MISHAPS

Friend: “So you’re saying that you were brought together by the FARC?”

Niku: “Well….I guess communism isn’t all bad.”

In a roundabout way, the Revolutionary Armed Forces of Colombia did bring us together. Our story begins with a series of mishaps. 

Mishap #1: I arranged our first date at a relatively popular Mexican restaurant in Downtown Philadelphia. Figuring that I didn’t need reservations for drinks on a weekday night we arrived to a completely full restaurant.

Mishap #2: Because I’m so quick on my feet and a radiologist with x-ray vision (get it?) I spotted a speakeasy across the street with a life-sized cutout of Shah Rukh Khan in it (true story). We had a fantastic few hours and Asha mentions to me that in a couple weeks she is going to Colombia for a bachelorette party. Don’t forget this fact - it’ll be important. Towards the end, I realized that Asha hadn’t eaten dinner yet (I’d eaten dinner because obviously you would never commit yourself to dinner on a first date). Luckily, Spice End was right next door and we were able to enjoy a few scrumptious kathi rolls (x-ray vision is awesome!).

Mishap #3 - this one is Asha’s fault: We both agreed that we had a fantastic time and agreed to meet up a second time. I eagerly wait the requisite three days (you know to not appear desperate) and text Asha. After waiting (the requisite) three hours she responds and we arrange a bowling alley date. The day arrives and Asha texts me saying that she’s sick at home with the flu. I told her to feel better and text me when she feels better.

6 MONTHS GO BY! (Dang girl you is sick!)

It’s Sunday morning, rain is falling (it could’ve been, don’t quite remember) and I’m reading the New York Times. I begin to read a story about a deal that the government of Colombia and the FARC have struck. And what strikes me is that Asha never contacted me. Out of nowhere, I decide to send her the article I was reading and ask her about the trip. And the rest is history.

There you have it, even though the FARC and Colombia never actually secured their union, I met the love of my life and we look forward to securing our union with all of you!

Asha: “Hey, the guy is supposed to reach out to the girl!!!!”
